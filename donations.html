Environmental Defense Fund
Works on issues relating to global warming, ecosystem restoration, oceans, and human health. Uses sound science (scientific research), law, and economics to figure out environmental solutions to fight global warming.
Global Footprint Network
Works with companies, governments, and individuals to help them make decisions that are wiser and ecologically safer. The Global Footprint Network has worked with 50 nations, 30 cities and 70 global partners to help fight climate change and invest in sustainable development.
Earth Day Network
Engages in projects that raise awareness on climate change, deforestation, poverty, and so much more. They are the reason why Earth Day exists.
